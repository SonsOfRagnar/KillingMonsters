Killing Monsters

A Monster of the Week style game of Dungeons & Dragons 5e, heavily inspired by the Witcher series by Andrzej Sapkowski, and the games of the same name by CD Projeckt Red. The players are a group of professional, itinerant monster hunters, roving from place to place looking for work, slaying beasts, haggling with distrustful locals, and occasionally tangling with court politics. Each contract they take involves investigating the area, speaking to witnesses, and identifying their quarry, before preparing themselves for the hunt.

To represent their combined experience and knowledge of monsters, the players have access to a Bestiary of the creatures they may face, describing their strengths, weaknesses, folklore, and other information that may be used when hunting them. Armed with this knowledge, the players must narrow down the potential danger to decide how best to prepare, collecting ingredients, brewing potions, and choosing their equipment wisely.
